Baked Baking Soda (Sodium Carbonate)

Sodium carbonate is a strong alkaline salt that is used in making ramen (noodles), among other foods. Heating baking soda makes it transform from sodium bicarbonate into sodium carbonate, which is a stronger alkaline salt.
