### Ingredients

* `1/2 cup` fresh lime juice
* `1` juice from lemon
* `1 cup` triple sec
* `1 cup` cheap, white tequila
* `2 cups` ice

### Directions

1. Blend. Pour into glasses rimmed with Kosher salt.
