### Ingredients

* `1 c.` Tanquery 10 Gin
* `1/2 c.` Grapefruit juice
* `1/2 c.` Rose's Sweetened lime juice

### Directions

1. Shake all ingredients together in a cocktail shaker with cracked ice.
