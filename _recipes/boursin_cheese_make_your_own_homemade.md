Adjust the flavorings to your taste - try other herbs like chives or basil. Use as a spread or to stuff mushrooms. Making your own Boursin allows you to taste the real garlic and herbs.

### Ingredients

* `2` garlic cloves
* `8 ounces` unsalted butter, softened
* `2 (8 ounce)` packages cream cheese, softened
* `½ teaspoon` salt
* `½ teaspoon` chopped fresh basil
* `½ teaspoon` marjoram, fresh or dried
* `½ teaspoon` chopped fresh chives
* `¼ teaspoon` thyme, fresh or dried
* `½ teaspoon` fresh ground black pepper
* `1 teaspoon` dill weed

### Directions

1. Add all ingredients to a blender or food processor and process until smooth and well combined.
2. Spoon mixture into a small serving bowl and chill overnight.
3. Serve at room temperature.
