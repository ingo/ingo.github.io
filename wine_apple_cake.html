Wine Apple Cake

by Hannelore Weigert 2023-08-03

A delightful German dessert, the Wine Apple Cake, is a wine-infused apple cake that combines the sweetness of apples with the rich flavor of wine.

Wine Apple Cake

  • Servings: 1 cake
  • Time: 1 hour

This recipe comes from Hannelore Weigert, a German baker known for her traditional recipes. The Wine Apple Cake is a classic German dessert that combines the sweetness of apples with the rich flavor of wine.

Ingredients

  • Shortcrust pastry (Mürbteig):
  • 125 g Butter
  • 125 g Sugar
  • 250 g Flour
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 pack Vanilla Sugar
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • Wine Apple Cake:
  • 3/4 L Wine (or 2 L Wine and 1/4 L Apple Juice)
  • 250 g Sugar
  • 2 packs Pudding Powder
  • 2 ½ Pounds Apples
  • 2 Cups Cream
  • Cocoa for dusting

Directions

  • Prepare the shortcrust pastry by combining butter, sugar, flour, egg, vanilla sugar, and baking powder. Press the dough into a springform pan.
  • For the Wine Apple Cake, cook a cream from wine, sugar, and pudding powder.
  • Clean and slice the apples, then fold them into the wine pudding. Spread this mixture over the shortcrust pastry.
  • Bake for about 30 minutes at 175° C (347° F).
  • Once the cake is baked and cooled, whip the cream and spread it over the cake. Dust with cocoa.
  • Enjoy your Wine Apple Cake!
